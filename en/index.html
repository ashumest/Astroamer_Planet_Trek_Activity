AstRoamer: Planet Trek

You have already learnt about the solar system. Now, test your knowledge by playing AstRoamer: Planet Trek.

Every planet in our solar system is made up of various elements and chemical compounds.

In this activity, you must find the elements and compounds using the clues given to you. The more items you collect, the better your chance of winning.

Are you ready?

Click the Rules button to read the rules of the activity.

Then, click Start to begin.
AstRoamer: Planet Trek: Rules
  • You will be given 10 clues, one at a time.
  • Read each clue and identify which planet it describes.
  • Click the correct planet and click Submit to check your answer.
  • If you answer correctly, you collect one element or compound.
  • The more items you find, the better your chances of winning.
Click X to continue.
